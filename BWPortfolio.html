ABOUT

HELLO, MY NAME IS BRITTNI WRIGHT,

I a full stack software engineer. I began to write code when I was in college at East Carolina University as a hobby. I graduated with a business science degree in economics. After a few years of doing economic research, I was unable to scratch my creative itch and decided to bring software engineering to the forefront. I attended the UNC Chapel-Hill advanced software engineer program to gain more knowledge on backend technologies and databases.

As a freelance web developer, I have been able to work directly with my clients to provide beautiful, robust and functional websites from scratch or by frameworks of their choice. I pride myself on being able to provide user interfaces that are unforgettable and magnificent. I have worked on a multitude of web and print based projects for a range of clients providing Web Design (Photoshop, Illustrator) and Development (Angular, HTML, CSS, LESS, Javascript, jQuery, WordPress, Materilize, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts).

In the last year, I have joined Fidelity Investments as a software engineer to gain more knowledge and experience working in the SDLC (software development life cycle). I still have clients that I maintain and I am always looking, willing and able to help new clients. I am always available to discuss your ideas in person, over the phone or via Skype.
